RA 8792- Electronic Commerce Act of 2000

OBJECTIVES

1. Promote electronic transactions public or private.
2. Recognize the validity of electronic documents.
3. Facilitate domestic and international dealings through ICT.
4. Provide legal recognition of electronic signatures.

IMPORTANCE

* Convenience wherein individuals can shop anytime, anywhere.
* Wider reach that helps businesses reach global markets easier.
* Lower cost kasi di na need i-consider physical stores.
* Payment convenience.

KEY PROVISIONS- What are included or recognized by this law.

1. Electronic Documents
2. E-signatures
3. E-contracts
4. Digital evidence are also considered valid in court.

PENAL PROVISIONS

1. Hacking- Unauthorized access to computer systems.
2. Piracy
3. Theft , Fraud \& Identity
4. Penalties
