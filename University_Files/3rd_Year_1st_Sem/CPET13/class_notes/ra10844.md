DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY ACT OF 2015 (RA10844)

- Passed in 2015 and signed into law on May 23, 2016
- President: Benigno S. Aquino III
  Senator: Frank Drilon
  Congressman: Feliciano Belmonte Jr.


DICT MANDATE
- DICT will now be the main government body to handle ICT related functions.

PROGRAMS AND PRIORITIES
- National Broadband Plan- Aims to improve infrastructure across the country.
- Free Wifi for All Program- Aims to bring internet access to all areas.
- National ICT Portal- Website that makes government services easier to access.
