History of Science and Technolgy in the Philippines

PRE COLONIAL PERIOD
- Agriculture
- Calendar
- Hervs remedy for illnesses
- Tools out of wood & Textile 
- Pottery and jewerly
- Banaue Rice Terraces
- These are all inidgenous or Folk Science

SPANISH PERIOD
- Established schools and introduced subjects and disciplines.
- Spanish Colonization (1565-1898)
- Introduction if European Technology to Philippines all accessible to the rich only
- Filipinoes who are not rich adapted and replicated such technologies. (local stone, bamboo, Nipa)
- Filipinoes adapted and modified such european technologies into farming tools that suit our tropical climate
- Medicine and advanced Science were introduced in formal colleges and universities established by Catholic orders (Jesuits, Dominicans, Franciscans) which taught medicines and advance science. 
- Galleon Trade has brought additional technology and development in the Philippines which ushered the Golden Age of Manila.  Lots of tools and crops and agriculture stuff and Filipinoes had little control on those trading systems.
