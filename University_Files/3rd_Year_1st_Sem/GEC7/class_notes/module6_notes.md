POST INDUSTRIAL SOCIETY:
- Development where exonomies move from manufacturing based systems to service, knowledge, and technology driven technology.
- Focus sa pag provide ng services such as information, education professional expertise, etc..
- Daniel Bell pipularized the concept of post industrial society in his book-Coming of post industrial society book, identifying 6 key features.
1. Shoft from goods to services.
2. Rise of technical to professional workers.
3. Replacement of practical knowledge with theoretical Knowledge.
4. Ethical and theoretical implication of technology.
5. Development of new scientific disciplines.
6. Increase of universities and institutions.
