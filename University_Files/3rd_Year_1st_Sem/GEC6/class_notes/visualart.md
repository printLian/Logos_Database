VISUAL ART
- art forms that express message, meaning and emotion.

AESTHETIC
- branch of philosophy concerned with feelings aroused by sensory experiences from the five senses.

REPRESENTATIONAL ART
- works of art copied from real life objects.

NON-REPRESENTATIONAL ART
- abstract. Opposite of representational art. 

PIGMENT
- group of compounds that are used to color other materials.

TYPES OF PAINTING MEDIUMS
1. Oil Painting- Use of oil paint by mixing pigment and dyring oil.
2. Watercolor Painting- Gum Arabic and pigment is what makes up watercolor paint.
3. Acrylic Paint
4. Gouache Paint- similar to watercolor but pigment is higher in content than gum Arabic.
5. Tempera Paint- Fast Drying paint.
